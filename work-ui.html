A series of website designs with a focus on UI

Duration 2019 - 2020
Overview These projects prioritize fulfilling the clients' business needs while featuring responsive website design and attractive, user-friendly interfaces.

NESCAFÉ®️ - Earning exciting prizes with canned coffee

NESCAFÉ®️ canned coffee has launched the reward program to celebrate the release of its new packaging and to motivate Hong Kongers who are facing work pressure. As part of this initiative, a dedicated campaign website has been created to provide customers with a seamless and engaging experience. By scanning the QR code on the can and visiting the website, customers can register the code on the can lid and earn points towards rewards. The website is easy to use and offers the latest updates on the program, along with information on how to participate and redeem rewards. Customers can enjoy rewards like free coffee, vouchers, and exclusive merchandise. The program promotes the brand's commitment to customer satisfaction.

Petty - Finding trusted pet sitters & house sits

Petty is an online platform that connects pet owners with trusted sitters and home boarders. The website provides a reliable and convenient way for pet owners to find suitable caregivers for their pets when they are away. By using Petty's search and filter functions, pet owners can easily find sitters or boarders based on their location, availability, and specific pet care requirements. Petty's user-friendly interface allows pet owners to browse through profiles of potential sitters, view photos and reviews, and communicate with them directly through the platform. Petty also provides a secure payment system, so pet owners can easily pay for the services they book through the website.

OKiBook - Booking restaurant with best price guaranteed

OKiBook is a comprehensive online platform that provides users with a hassle-free way to explore a wide range of dining options in Hong Kong and Macau. The website features an extensive list of restaurants, bars, and other food venues, all of which are carefully curated to offer the best dining experiences to users. By using OKiBook's search and filter functions, users can easily find dining destinations based on their food preferences, budget, location, and other specific needs. Moreover, the website is regularly updated with the latest restaurant news, promotions, and special offers, ensuring that users have access to the most up-to-date information when making their dining decisions.

KAB Blue - Featuring a user-friendly trading platform

The website is user-friendly, featuring a simplified trading platform and tools that empower users to easily buy and sell stocks, exchange-traded funds, options, and other securities across multiple markets. Furthermore, it provides a wealth of investment research and analysis, such as market news, data, charts, and expert insights, to assist users in making informed investment decisions and achieving their investment goals.

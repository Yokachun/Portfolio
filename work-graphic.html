Graphic, illustration and multimedia design

Duration 2015 - 2019
Objective Engaging in self-initiated projects is an excellent avenue to flex my creative muscle, explore various techniques, and showcase my skills to the world.

Graphic design - Design dictionary

Designed for beginners, this vocabulary teaches 31 essential design theories through clear and simple verbal and visual explanations. By providing an easy-to-understand introduction to the basics of design, this dictionary aims to inspire readers to deepen their knowledge and pursue further learning in this exciting field.

31 essential design theories

Character design - Mushroom baby

With her optimistic, imaginative, and childlike attitude, Mushroom Baby is a beloved character known for her many cosplay outfits and appearances as a background actor or extra performer. Whether she's on stage or behind the scenes, this endearing character always brings a touch of magic to any production.

Mushroom baby cosplays as the twelve constellations

Photography - Hidden beauty

This photo album showcases the hidden beauty of everyday objects, revealing their unique forms, colors, and tones that often go unnoticed. By encouraging people to explore the aesthetic pleasures of common items, I hope to inspire a greater appreciation for the world around us.

A photo album of beautiful discoveries

Game design - Horizon milk

This engaging game provides a fun experience for young customers, while also conveying the message that Horizon milk can aid in calcium retention, promoting strength and overall health. Players are sure to have an unforgettable time while learning about the benefits of this nutritious beverage.
